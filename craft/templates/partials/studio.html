Bokeh was born out of a promise to never stop creating.

David and Doug met in film school. David liked to tell stories, and Doug liked to play with cameras.

So in 2011 they started a company to do just that.

They did wedding videos, engagements, even maternity videos. Those were weird.

They both learned a lot about marketing, design, branding, and content. Then things started to happen. Fast. All over the world. The work piled up.

David was overwhelmed. He asked his best friend Sebastian for help. He was organized and good with numbers.

So Sebastian joined the company, and in 2013 Bokeh was born.
